<p>We would like to congratulate 2 of our staff for receiving their National
Day Award this year. Congratulations to our Senior Teacher Mr Tan Kok Meng
for receiving his Long Service Medal. We would also like to congratulate
Mr Velayutham Raj for his Commendation Medal.</p>
<p>The Long Service Medal may be awarded to any person who is of irreproachable
character and whom the President is satisfied has completed at least 25
years of service in the service.</p>
<p>The Commendation Medal may be awarded to persons who have distinguished
themselves through commendable performance and conduct, or significant
efficiency, competence and devotion to duty.</p>
