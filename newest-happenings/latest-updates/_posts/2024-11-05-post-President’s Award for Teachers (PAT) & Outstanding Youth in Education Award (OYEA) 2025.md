<p>Dear Parents/Guardians,</p>
<p>We are thrilled to announce that nominations are now open for two distinguished
national awards celebrating outstanding educators: the President’s Award
for Teachers (PAT) 2025 and the Outstanding Youth in Education Award (OYEA)
2025.</p>
<p>Do you know a teacher who has made a meaningful difference in your child’s
education? This is your chance to show your appreciation for their dedication
and impact by nominating them for one of these prestigious awards!</p>
<p>President’s Award for Teachers (PAT) 2025</p>
<p>This award honours experienced educators who are exemplary role models
in the teaching profession. These teachers embrace innovative teaching
methods, are committed to lifelong learning, and actively mentor their
colleagues. Their passion for teaching and ability to inspire students
and peers make them deserving of this recognition.</p>
<p>Outstanding Youth in Education Award (OYEA) 2025</p>
<p>OYEA celebrates young teachers (35 and under) who embody youthful energy
and enthusiasm in their teaching. These educators serve as inspiring role
models for students and colleagues alike, bringing vibrancy and dedication
to their work.</p>
<p>You can submit nominations for both awards online at the links below:</p>
<p>PAT Nomination: <a href="https://go.gov.sg/pat2025" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/pat2025</a>
</p>
<p>OYEA Nomination: <a href="https://go.gov.sg/oyea2025" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/oyea2025</a>
</p>
<p>Nominations close on 10 January 2025, so don’t miss this opportunity to
recognise the exceptional efforts of our teachers.</p>
<p>Thank you for helping us celebrate the educators who are shaping the future
of our children!</p>
