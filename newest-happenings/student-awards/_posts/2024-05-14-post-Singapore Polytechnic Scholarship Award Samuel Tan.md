<p>Singapore Polytechnic (SP) is delighted that our former student, Samuel
Tan, has been selected for this year's <strong>Singapore Polytechnic Scholarship Award</strong>.</p>
<p>This award recognizes students with excellent academic achievements, outstanding
leadership qualities, and significant accomplishments in co-curricular
activities.&nbsp;</p>
<p>SP believe that Samuel Tan’s achievements will inspire the students of
Mayflower Secondary School to strive for greater heights not just academically
but also in their co-curricular activities.</p>
<p>SP is proud of Samuel Tan’s accomplishments and believes that he will
continue to make both SP and Mayflower Secondary School proud.</p>
