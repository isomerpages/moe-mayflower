<h1>2024年全国课文朗诵比赛</h1>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/7th_National_Text_Recital_Competition.png">
</div>
<p>The 7th National Text Recital Competition witnessed outstanding performances
from Mayflowerians, who showcased their exceptional skills in Chinese text
recitation. This prestigious event, organized by the Committee to Promote
Chinese Language Learning (CPCLL), Pei Hwa Presbyterian Primary School,
and the Singapore Chinese Teachers Literary Recital Society, aims to enhance
students' proficiency in Chinese and foster a deeper appreciation for the
language's nuances, rhythm, and beauty.</p>
<p>In the Lower Secondary Individual category, Celeste Wong (2A) secured
an impressive third place, demonstrating her remarkable dedication in learning
the Chinese Language.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 30%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/National_Text_Recital_Comp_02.jpg">
</div>
<p>In the Lower Secondary Group category, Chia Pei En (2B), Luo Yafei (2D)
and Tao Yixuan (2I) delivered a confident performance that helped them
surpass other contestants and emerged champion! More importantly, they
enjoyed the journey and the experience, highlighting the spirit of teamwork
and camaraderie.</p>

<p>Mayflowerians' success at the competition underscores the school's commitment
to developing each student to the best of their interests and abilities.</p>
